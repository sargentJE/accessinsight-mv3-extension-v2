## 4. Accuracy and Limitations

### 4.1 Accuracy Disclaimer

AccessInsight uses automated scanning to detect accessibility issues. While we strive for accuracy:

- **Not 100% accurate**: Based on testing, AccessInsight achieves approximately 82.7% precision
- **False positives**: Some reported issues may not be actual violations (~17%)
- **False negatives**: Some issues may not be detected
- **Manual review required**: Many accessibility criteria require human judgment

### 4.2 Scope Limitations

AccessInsight tests **automated-testable** WCAG criteria only. Many WCAG Success Criteria require manual evaluation:

**Not covered by automation**:
- Meaningful content sequence
- Quality of alt text (presence is checked, but not meaning)
- Accuracy of ARIA labels
- Keyboard functionality beyond focus
- Context-dependent compliance issues
- Cognitive accessibility aspects

**Coverage**:
- ✅ 46 automated rules covering major WCAG 2.1 and 2.2 criteria
- ⚠️ Some manual review required for complete compliance

### 4.3 No Guarantee of Compliance

**Using AccessInsight does NOT guarantee**:
- Legal compliance with accessibility laws (ADA, Section 508, etc.)
- WCAG conformance certification
- Protection from accessibility-related lawsuits
- Complete coverage of all accessibility issues

**You are responsible for**:
- Manual testing and review
- Professional accessibility audits (when required)
- Legal compliance in your jurisdiction
- Fixing identified issues
